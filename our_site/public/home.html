We are Tick.et

We are a student run ticketing website that mainly supports performing art societies from the University of Bristol. We have shows running at our most popular venues, namely Winston Theatre and Pegg Studio, throughout the year.
Please contact an admin if you would like to create a ticket listing for your production on our site.
